### Issue 2 — Calibration Pulse and Annotations Required in Section 2.2 but Absent from Section 2.3 Implementation Sketch
**RRC Severity:** REQUIRED
**EPM Decision:** FIXED IN DOCUMENT

Section 2.3 updated with four mandatory rendering constants:
- `CALIBRATION_PULSE_MV = 1.0` — 1 mV square wave at start of each row
- `CALIBRATION_PULSE_DURATION_MS = 200` — 200 ms wide (5 small squares at 25 mm/s)
- `SHOW_SPEED_GAIN_ANNOTATION = True` — "25 mm/s  10 mm/mV" printed bottom-left
- `SHOW_LEAD_LABELS = True` — lead name printed upper-left of each strip

Rationale comment added: "Without calibration pulse: VL2 cannot anchor amplitude scale. Without speed annotation: VL2 may assume non-standard paper speed. Without lead labels: VL2 must infer lead identity from position only."

Issue resolved.
